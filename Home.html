HISTORY OF DEPARTMENT

The Department of computer science is striving hard towards the goal of providing innovative and quality education with high standard to achieve academic excellence and provides platform for the students to achieve their career goals. With Tamso Maa Jyotirgamaya as its guiding principle, the Vidarbha Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Khamgaon founded the Govindram Seksariya Science College in 1947 with donation given by Late Seth Govindram Seksaria of Indore in order to cultivate the values of modesty, brotherhood, devotion, courage, patriotism, discipline, sacrifice, patience and determination in the youth of the post-independence era. The late Advocate Raobahadur Anant Sakharam Athalye of Akola was the first president of the VSP Mandal while there were some prominent citizens of Khamgaon among the founder members. The VSP Mandal is registered under B.P.T. Act, 1950 with registration number F-1 Buldana. In 1954, Khamgaon evolved into G. S. Science, Arts and Commerce College, Khamgaon.

We have started Computer Science in 1987, with two graduate courses BSc Computer Science and Computer Application Vocational. Also in 2007 we have started offering the graduate courses in BCA. As well as started offering post graduate course MSc in Computer Science. The college has its own spacious building with total campus area of 99 acres. There are well-equipped and modern laboratories and a library enriched with more than 60,000 books and recently G.S. College Khamgaon has been given A Great by NAAC committee.This is the biggest achievement in the history of college as well as Computer Science Department. Because this department played very important role in that achievement. Thus,this is oldest college in the western part of the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra is continuously treading the path of academic excellence.
