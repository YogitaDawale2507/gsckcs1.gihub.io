Bachelor of Science(Computer Science)

The 3 years BSc Computer Science course allows the candidates to learn about the different architectures related to computer networking, operating system, and programming languages. One Key advantage of the course is that the candidates can save one year of their career as compared to the engineering course.BSc Computer Science deals with the theoretical knowledge and skills in the field of computer science the real world for various uses.

Bachelor of Computer Application

Bachelors in Computer Application (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree course for students who wish to delve into the world of Computer languages. The BCA course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology. A degree in BCA is at par with a B.Tech/B.E degree in Computer Science or Information Technology. For a BCA aspirant, this degree would help in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications.

Bachelor of Science(Computer Application Vocational)

Computer Applications vocational is a 3-year full-time undergraduate degree program dealing with the study related to the various aspects of computer functioning. During this course study, the students can get in-depth knowledge on Windows, Programming Languages, Operating System, Web Designing and more.

Criteria

The basic eligibility criteria is completing 10+2 level in Science stream or DOEACC Level 3 or equivalent degree from a recognized educational board with a minimum 50% aggregate score.
This course is specially designed to create quality IT professionals who are able to solve any critical IT issues in the software industry.
